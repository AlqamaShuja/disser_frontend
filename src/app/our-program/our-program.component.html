Being A Good Residential Carer We Provide!

Emotional Regulation Techniques

Teaching children's skills and techniques to manage emotions, cope with stressors, and regulate behaviour through mindfulness, relaxation exercises, and social-emotional learning programs.

Therapeutic Support

Offering individual and group therapy sessions with qualified counsellors or psychologists to address emotional issues, trauma, anxiety, depression, anger management, and self-esteem.

Creating A Nurturing Environment

Establishing safe and supportive homes for children with special needs, prioritizing emotional well-being, stability, and personalised care plans to foster growth and development.

Specialised Care Plans

Implementing personalised care strategies tailored to each child's unique needs, emphasising emotional support, stability, and holistic development within our residential setting.

Transition Planning For Children With Specialised Needs

Transition planning for children receiving residential care, experiencing learning difficulties, or emotional behavioural disorders involves creating tailored plans for their transition into adulthood and independence.

Council Liaison And Support Coordination

Collaborating closely with local councils to ensure comprehensive support and coordination for children, including regular updates and involvement in care planning.
